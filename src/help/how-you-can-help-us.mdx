If you enjoy Diarly and would like to help us grow:

- Spread the word about Diarly -  we are a small start-up company so our growth depends highly on the recommendations.

- Rate Diarly in the App Store or other websites which as such option (e.g. [https://alternativeto.net/](https://alternativeto.net))

- Suggest improvements or report issues - Diarly has a built-in feedback form, but you can also contact us via email and twitter messages.

- Consider becoming Premium subscriber
