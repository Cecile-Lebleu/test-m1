Diarly uses Apple iTunes for handling purchases in the app. Learn how to [see or cancel your subscriptions](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202039) on different Apple devices.

The subscription is valid on all the devices where you are logged with the same iCloud account, and it will show the premium version of Diarly.
