With Share as Image, you can automatically generate a beautiful image with your favorite thoughts or quotes, ready to share to social media in seconds.

1. Select the text you want to share.
2. Right-click on the selected text, and select "Share as Image". Alternatively, click on `Edit > Share as Image`.
3. Tune to your preferences, and hit Share on the top right.

In the options, you can select between different image sizes specific to different social media: Facebook, Instagram post, Instagram story, Twitter, Linkedin, Pinterest, or a square 2048Ã—2048 image.

You can also select from any of the available themes (over 100 themes in Premium!), and select any font from your computer.
